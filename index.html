What is Pelle Pelle?

Pelle Pelle is an iconic urban fashion brand founded by Marc Buchanan in 1978. Renowned for its groundbreaking approach to streetwear, Pelle Pelle revolutionized the fashion world with its oversized silhouettes, bold branding, and exceptional craftsmanship. Known for its premium leather jackets, Pelle Pelle has become a cultural phenomenon and a symbol of hip-hop fashion.
